> **翻闸机制**：`CatalogFactory.createCatalog`（`datasource/CatalogFactory.java`）——`SPI_READY_TYPES.contains(type)` 且有 ConnectorProvider → 建 `PluginDrivenExternalCatalog`；否则 legacy。现 `SPI_READY_TYPES`={jdbc,es,trino-connector,max_compute,paimon}（`:50-51`），iceberg **不在**。翻闸 = 加 "iceberg"。
